About Us

Echoes of Eternity is an independent game development project. Our team is dedicated to creating an immersive gaming experience with a rich storyline, engaging characters, and stunning visuals. Therefore "Echoes of Eternity", which is a story-driven adventure game that delves into the mysteries of time and the human condition.

We believe in pushing the boundaries of what is possible in game development, and we're committed to delivering a high-quality gaming experience to our players.

Background

In a world where time has no bounds, the fabric of reality is threatened by a cataclysmic event known as "The Great Dissonance". This event has shattered the balance of the timestream, causing echoes of the past, present, and future to collide.
